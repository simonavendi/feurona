helt.ty

App Design — Здраве. Ежедневно. Ти.

helt.ty — Здраве. Ежедневно. Ти. is a Bulgarian health & wellness mobile app concept I designed with a calm, iOS-inspired minimal UI. The product helps people track daily habits — steps, calories, water, workouts, and meals — in one clear dashboard built for the local market.

I shaped the experience across four core areas: a home dashboard with progress rings for steps, calories, and hydration; activity tracking with weekly charts and recent workouts; a food diary with macro breakdowns (calories, protein, carbs, fats); and profile & settings for health data, notifications, and privacy. The visual system uses Manrope, a light #F8F9FF background, and primary green #50d130 — clinical enough to trust with health data, warm enough to encourage daily use.
