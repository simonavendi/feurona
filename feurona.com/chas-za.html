Час За

App Design — UX/UI & Branding

Час За (Chas Za) is a Bulgarian appointment-booking app I designed end to end — from brand identity and logo through every screen in the product. The goal was a calm, trustworthy mobile experience that helps service businesses manage schedules and lets clients book in a few taps.

I owned the full UX/UI process: user flows, onboarding, business and client profiles, service setup, scheduling, notifications, payments, and account recovery. The visual language stays light and approachable — clear hierarchy, generous spacing, and UI patterns tuned for local salons, studios, and small businesses.
